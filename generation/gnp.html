Random Erdős–Rényi network¶

C++:

template<dag::integer_vertex VertT, std::uniform_random_bit_generator Gen>
dag::undirected_network<VertT> dag::random_gnp_graph(VertT size, double p, Gen &generator)¶

Python:

dag.random_gnp_graph[vert_type](size: int, p: float,    random_state) -> dag.undirected_network[vert_type]

Generates a random \(G(n = size, p)\) graph of size size, where every edge exists independently with probability p [1].
